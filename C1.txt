Slide 2: Background and Context
The Hidden Cost of Customer Churn
• Customer churn is one of the largest hidden costs in retail banking.
• Losing existing customers leads to:
• Reduced Lifetime Value
• Increased customer acquisition costs
• Revenue instability
• Banks have rich customer data but lack granular segmentation insights.
• Key unanswered questions:
• Which customer groups are most likely to churn?
• How does churn differ across countries (France, Spain, Germany), age groups, and financial profiles?
• Is churn concentrated among high-value customers?

Slide 3: Problem Statement
Current Challenges
Despite rich customer-level data, banks face challenges in:
• Identifying high-risk customer segments
• Understanding churn differences by geography and demographics
• Quantifying the financial profile of churned customers
Impact:
Without structured analytics, churn management strategies remain generic, reactive, and inefficient.

Slide 4: Project Objectives
Primary Objectives:
    • Measure overall churn rate
    • Identify churn distribution across customer segments
    • Compare churn behavior across European regions (France, Spain, Germany) Secondary Objectives:
    • Understand churn among high-value customers
    • Evaluate engagement and tenure patterns
    • Support strategic planning and marketing decisions

Slide 7: Key Findings (with Fake Data)
Major Insights:
• Overall Churn Rate: 21.4%
• Highest Churn by Country: Germany (26.8%)
• Highest Churn by Age Group: 46-60 years (28.5%)
• High-Value Customers Churn Rate: 18.2%
• Low Tenure Customers (<2 years): 34.7% churn
Risk Segments: German males aged 46-60 with medium credit scores and low activity.

Slide 8: Recommendations & Conclusion
Recommendations:
1. Targeted retention campaigns for high-risk segments (esp. Germany)
2. Improve engagement for new and inactive customers
3. Personalized offers for high-value customers
4. Early warning predictive models
Conclusion:
This project delivers actionable, segmentation-driven insights to reduce churn and strengthen customer retention in European banking.
